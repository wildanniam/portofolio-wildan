## Asset safety rules

- Figma Community and React-library assets are researched only in the later asset phase.
- Every external resource requires a recorded license and attribution decision.
- Community assets may establish a technique or production shortcut; they must not replace Wildan's authentic project evidence or become the portfolio concept.
- Generated imagery must never impersonate a real product screenshot, award, person, event, test, transaction, partner, or user result.
- Demo/seeded data is labeled in captions and cannot be used as traction evidence.
- Personal and team photographs remain unpublished until credit and consent are ready.
- Original photographs stay outside the workspace/repository; only cropped, redacted, EXIF-stripped, optimized, and explicitly approved derivatives enter `public/media`.
- Broken or incomplete live states can appear as honest engineering evidence, but never as an unlabeled polished outcome.

## Release rule

A case study can be structurally implemented before this backlog is complete, but it is not visually final until it has authentic evidence for product reality, system reasoning, and verification; a limitations frame; and all relevant rights/provenance metadata. Nova, PayGate, and Quorum now satisfy the three evidence functions in protected preview. They remain `preview` until checkpoint D approves the exact derivatives, and Nova also requires final award-photo credit/consent. No fixed screenshot count is required.
